Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order quantity varies by product type. For standard items, we typically require a minimum order of 1 ton. For custom fabrication, the MOQ depends on the complexity of the project. Contact our sales team for specific requirements.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we export our steel products to various international markets. We handle all necessary documentation and logistics to ensure smooth delivery to your location. Shipping costs and timelines depend on the destination and order volume.

What quality certifications do you have?

We are ISO 9001:2015 certified for quality management systems. Our products also comply with international standards including ASTM, BS, and IS specifications. We can provide material test certificates for all our products upon request.

How long does custom fabrication take?

Custom fabrication timelines depend on the complexity and volume of the order. Standard custom projects typically take 2-4 weeks from approval of technical drawings to completion. Larger or more complex projects may require additional time.
