About SteelWorks

With over 18 years of excellence in the steel industry, we are committed to providing high-quality steel solutions that build the foundations of modern infrastructure.

Our Story

Founded in 2005, SteelWorks began as a small family-owned business with a vision to revolutionize the steel supply industry. What started as a modest operation with just 10 employees has now grown into a leading steel solutions provider with a team of over 200 dedicated professionals.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, strategic expansion, and an unwavering commitment to quality. From our first major contract supplying structural steel for commercial buildings to our current position as a trusted partner for industrial and infrastructure projects nationwide, we've maintained our core values of integrity, excellence, and customer satisfaction.

Today, we operate from a state-of-the-art 50,000 sq. ft. facility equipped with advanced manufacturing technology, allowing us to serve diverse sectors including construction, automotive, infrastructure, and industrial manufacturing.
